Frequently Asked Questions

How does your delivery software work?

Our system uses GPS tracking and AI route optimization to ensure efficient deliveries. Customers can track orders in real-time, while our drivers get optimized routes that save time and fuel. The system also predicts demand based on historical usage patterns.

What makes your water different from competitors?

We use a 7-stage purification process followed by precise mineral enhancement to create the optimal balance for health and taste. Our water contains essential minerals like calcium, magnesium, and potassium in carefully measured amounts.

What are the investment terms?

We offer flexible investment options ranging from $50,000 to $500,000 with equity stakes from 10% to 40% depending on amount and terms. Typical investment horizon is 3-5 years with exit options including buyback or acquisition.

How do you ensure water quality?

We implement rigorous quality control including daily water testing, automated monitoring systems, and regular facility inspections. Our plant meets all PSQCA standards and we're working towards international certifications.
