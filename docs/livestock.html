Our Butdarian Livestock Farm

Located in the fertile lands of Butdarian, Mansehra, our livestock farm specializes in raising premium quality animals for Eid and commercial markets. With years of experience and modern farming techniques, we ensure each animal receives the best care, nutrition, and living conditions.

Our farm spans over 10 acres of lush green pastures, providing the perfect environment for healthy livestock growth. We take pride in our ethical farming practices and commitment to animal welfare, resulting in superior quality livestock that commands premium prices in the market.

Our Farming Process

Breeding & Selection

We carefully select breeding stock from premium bloodlines to ensure quality offspring. Our animals are chosen for their health, size, and genetic traits that make them ideal for both meat and dairy purposes.

Feeding Program

Our animals enjoy a balanced diet of natural grasses from our pastures supplemented with specially formulated feed that includes:

  • Protein-rich legumes and grains
  • Mineral supplements for bone development
  • Seasonal fruits and vegetables
  • Clean, fresh water available at all times

Health Management

  • Regular deworming and vaccination schedules
  • 24/7 access to veterinary care
  • Disease prevention protocols
  • Clean and hygienic living conditions

Eid Preparation

As Eid ul Adha approaches, we implement special programs to ensure our animals are in peak condition:

  • Additional nutritional supplements
  • Weight monitoring and optimization
  • Grooming and cleaning
  • Stress reduction techniques

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase animals from your farm?

You can visit our farm in Butdarian to select animals in person, or contact us to discuss your requirements. For Eid, we recommend booking in advance as demand is high.

Do you offer delivery for purchased animals?

Yes, we can arrange safe transportation for animals to locations in Mansehra and surrounding areas. Delivery charges depend on distance and number of animals.

What makes your livestock different from others?

Our focus on premium breeding stock, scientific feeding programs, and exceptional veterinary care results in healthier, heavier animals that command better prices in the market.

Can I visit the farm to see the animals?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors to our Butdarian farm. Please call ahead to schedule your visit so we can ensure someone is available to show you around.

Do you offer guidance on animal care after purchase?

Yes, we provide complete care instructions and are available for consultation after purchase. We want to ensure the animals continue to thrive in their new homes.
