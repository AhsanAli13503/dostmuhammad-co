Our Livestock Farming Services

At our Butdarian farm in Mansehra, we specialize in raising premium quality livestock including Dumba sheep, goats, cows and buffaloes. Our modern farming techniques ensure healthy animals that command premium prices in the market, especially during Eid seasons.

With years of experience in livestock management, we've perfected the art of animal care, nutrition, and breeding to deliver superior quality livestock for both Eid sacrifices and commercial purposes.

Premium Breeding

Scientific Feeding

Health Management

Our Farming Process

Breeding & Selection

We carefully select breeding stock from premium bloodlines to ensure quality offspring. Our animals are chosen for their health, size, and genetic traits.

Our animals enjoy a balanced diet of natural grasses from our pastures supplemented with specially formulated feed:

Health Management

  • Disease prevention protocols
  • Clean and hygienic living conditions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase animals from your farm?

You can visit our farm in Butdarian to select animals in person, or contact us to discuss your requirements. For Eid, we recommend booking in advance as demand is very high during this period.

Do you offer delivery for purchased animals?

Yes, we can arrange safe transportation for animals to locations in Mansehra and surrounding areas. Delivery charges depend on distance and the number of animals being transported.

What makes your livestock different from others?

Our focus on premium breeding stock, scientific feeding programs, and exceptional veterinary care results in healthier, heavier animals that command premium prices in the market. We also provide complete health records for all our animals.

Can I visit the farm to see the animals?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors to our Butdarian farm. Please call ahead to schedule your visit so we can ensure someone is available to give you a proper tour and answer all your questions.

Do you offer bulk discounts for commercial buyers?

Yes, we offer special pricing for restaurants, meat processors, and other commercial buyers purchasing in quantity. Contact us directly to discuss your specific needs and volume requirements.
